## 1.Aha Note
Since 1970, business productivity in mature economies has barely moved, compared to the change in the processing power of computers. 
Over that period of time, business productivity has merely doubled, whereas the processing power of computers is 20 million times greater!

Solow Paradox
"You can see the computer age everywhere but in the productivity statistics."
